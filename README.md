## Simple Interest Calculator

A simple, user-friendly command-line calculator that computes the simple interest earned or owed over a specific period. This tool is designed to help users quickly determine interest outcomes given the foundational financial parameters.

### How It Works
The application takes three essential inputs from the user, processes them using the standard financial interest formula, and outputs the total accumulated simple interest.

### Formula Used
The calculator performs the calculation based on the following formula:

$$A = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100}$$

Where:
* **$P$ (Principal amount):** The initial sum of money invested or borrowed.
* **$R$ (Annual rate of interest):** The percentage rate charged or earned per year.
* **$T$ (Time period):** The duration of the loan or investment expressed in years.

---

## Usage Guide

### Input Parameters:
1. **`p` (Principal):** Enter the starting balance (e.g., 1000).
2. **`t` (Time):** Enter the total duration in years (e.g., 5).
3. **`r` (Rate):** Enter the annual interest percentage rate (e.g., 5 for 5%).

### Output:
* **Simple Interest:** The exact financial amount generated from the parameters provided.

---

## Example Calculation

To demonstrate the functionality, consider the following scenario:

* **Input:**
  * Principal Amount ($P$) = 1000
  * Time Period ($T$) = 2 years
  * Annual Interest Rate ($R$) = 5%

* **Calculation:**
  $$A = \frac{1000 \times 5 \times 2}{100} = 100$$

* **Output:**
  * `Simple Interest = 100`
